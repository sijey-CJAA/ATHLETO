ATHLETO SPORTS STORE PRIVACY POLICY

PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY CAREFULLY. By accessing or using ATHLETO's website, mobile app, or services (collectively, the "Platform"), you agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your personal data as described in this policy.

1. INTRODUCTION

ATHLETO Sports Store ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Data You Provide

  • Identity Data: Name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact Data: Email, phone number, shipping/billing address
  • Account Data: Username, password, profile information
  • Transaction Data: Payment details, order history, returns
  • Fitness Data: Workout preferences, sizing information (if provided)

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, session duration
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Location Data: Approximate location for store finder/delivery

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your information to:

  • Process orders and deliver products
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our products and Platform
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. DATA SHARING

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping carriers, IT support
  • Business Partners: Brands for exclusive offers (with consent)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Personalize your experience

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

6. DATA SECURITY

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for transactions
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You may:

  • Access or update your account information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)

Contact us at privacy@athleto.com to exercise these rights.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Platform is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.

9. POLICY UPDATES

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or Platform notice.
